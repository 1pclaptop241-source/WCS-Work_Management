# WiseCut Studios - User Guide

## 1. Getting Started
### Login & Security
- **Access**: Navigate to the login page and enter your email/password.
- **Password Visibility**: Click the "Eye" icon in the password field to show/hide your password.
- **Change Password**: Once logged in, click your profile icon (top right) -> "Change Password" to update your credentials securely.

## 2. For Admins
### Dashboard Overview
The Admin Dashboard is your central command center.
- **Unified Grid**: See all projects in a responsive card layout.
- **Filtering**: Use the color-coded buttons to filter:
  - `All Projects`: View everything.
  - `Pending` (Amber): Projects waiting for your review/acceptance.
  - `Active` (Blue): Projects currently in progress.
  - `Completed` (Green): Finished projects.
- **Search**: Use the search bar to find projects by **Title** or **Client Name**.

### Project Management
- **Creating Projects**:
  1. Click **"New Project"**.
  2. Fill in Title, Description, Client, and Deadline.
  3. **Important**: The deadline must be at least **15 minutes** in the future.
- **Accepting Work**: Go to the **Pending** tab. Click **"Review & Accept"** on a project card to move it to the pipeline.
- **Managing Work**: Click **"Manage Project"** on any active card to assign editors, view files, or share details.

### Performance Insights
- Click the **"Insights"** button (top right) to view Editor Performance stats.

## 3. For Clients
### Dashboard
- **Create Project**: Click "Create Project". **Note**: You cannot set a deadline less than **15 minutes** from the current time.
- **Track Progress**: View the status of your requested videos.
- **Approvals**: Review submitted work and provide feedback or approve it instantly.

## 4. For Editors
### Dashboard
- **My Work**: See all assigned tasks.
- **Deadlines**: Timers show how much time is remaining. Overdue projects are highlighted in Red.
- **Submissions**: Upload your finished files. The system automatically notifies the admin.
